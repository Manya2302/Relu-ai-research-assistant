# Competitor Analysis Implementation

The Competitor Analysis module (`backend/app/services/competitor_service.py` & `serper_service.py`) is responsible for identifying, validating, and enriching data about rival companies operating in the same domain.

## Architecture & Flow

1. **Discovery via AI & Search**
   Competitors are sourced via a two-pronged approach:
   - **AI Inference**: Based on the crawled products and website text, the LLM is instructed to identify 5 highly specific domain peers (avoiding generic responses like "Google" or "Microsoft" unless explicitly applicable).
   - **Serper Discovery**: The `SerperService` actively runs queries against Google (e.g., `"{Company Name} {Industry} {Country} competitors"`) to find market research lists and alternative providers.

2. **Validation & Enrichment**
   Once the initial list of competitor names is generated by the AI, it is passed to the `CompetitorService.enrich_competitors()` method.
   - For every competitor name, the system queries the Serper API.
   - It searches specifically for the competitor's official website.
   - It extracts a short snippet/description of what the competitor does.
   
3. **Structuring the Output**
   The final competitor objects are constructed to include:
   - `name`: The verified name of the competitor.
   - `website`: The exact URL to their homepage (crucial for users who want to click through and verify).
   - `reason`: A dynamically generated explanation of *why* they compete (e.g., "Both offer cloud-based EV engineering solutions").

4. **Integration into PDF**
   The enriched competitor list is finally passed to the `PDFService`, which generates a clean, tabular breakdown in the final report, categorizing the competitive threat level and linking directly to their domains.
